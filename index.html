Biography

I am a PhD Student in the School of Computer, Data, and Information Sciences at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am a research assistant in the MadS&P computer security group and advised by Dr. Patrick McDaniel. My research interests lie at the intersection of computer security and reinforcement learning. In current work, I’m exploring the space of transferability in reinforcement learning and how we can extract pre-trained representations to accelerate learning a new task.
